I joined the American Library Association (ALA) in 2005 while I was still in graduate school. I didn't become more involved in the association until 2008, when I volunteered for some committee work with NMRT. I always struggled with ALA involvement because of my historic lack of resources to attend ALA conferences. I was able to find some committee assignments that did not require conference attendance, which was perfect.

I am very honored to have been chosen as a 2012 ALA Emerging Leader! I am confident this will be a great way for me to get more deeply involved in ALA, which is a benefit to both myself and the association. I look forward to where this opportunity takes me.

Service

New Members Round Table (NMRT), 2005 - present

  • Web Committee, 2011 - present
    This committee keeps the NMRT website up to date. I received training on both the old Collage system and the new Drupal system. I make edits to pages as requested by NMRT committee chairs in my assigned area (Member Services).
  • Archives Committee, 2008 - 2011
    The Archives Committee has the important task of getting all NMRT committee and board member reports into a database for future access and searchability. My background in archives work drew me to this committee.
  • Mentoring Committee, 2008 - 2009
    This committee oversees the NMRT Mentoring Program. I had a group of mentor/mentee pairs for which I was responsible. I introduced them to each other, gave them a discussion topic once a month, and served as their point-person if problems came up during their time together.

Library Information Technology Association (LITA), 2007 - present

  • Web Coordinating Committee, 2012 - present
    This committee keeps the LITA's various web presences up to date.
  • Christian Larew Scholarship Committee, 2012 - present

ALA Emerging Leaders Program, 2012
