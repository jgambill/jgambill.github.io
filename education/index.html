Formal Education

M.S. Information Science
University of Tennessee-Knoxville, Knoxville, TN
2007

I completed my degree via UTK-SIS's distance education program, only stepping onto campus for orientation, for my comprehensive exam, and for graduation (optional). It was a great program in that I didn’t have to uproot my family or leave my library job to complete my degree. I truly do not feel like I missed out on anything by not being on campus – I learned a lot and made both professional contacts and great friends. After graduation, I served on the UTK SIS Alumni Board in 2009 – 2010, at which time the board was disbanded.
